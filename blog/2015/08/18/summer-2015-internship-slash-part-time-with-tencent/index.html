Summer 2015 Internship/Part-time With Tencent

During the summer of 2015, I had this chance working with Tencent Hangzhou team. This journal will mainly be about this experience.

Tencent Hangzhou is a, relatively-speaking, small team. We had about less than 50 people working full-time. Yet, majority (all except 3 people) of them are developers. The main business done there are still CONFIDENTIAL (I signed the contract that I should not talk about it in public domain), yet I was surprised to find out that I could talk about my project (I guess that means my project is not important maybe?)
