Project Ivy Init

Jun 3rd, 2014

I met with my girlfriend Ivy when we were both students in University of Illinois at Urbana Champaign. She was a master candidate majoring in Econ, and I was a 屌丝(Diaosi, Chinese Charactors, roughly translate into “a nerdy guy who everyone knows will not get a girl friend”) in Math & Computer Science.

Oct 2 of 2014 is our 1st year anniversary, and right now we are on skype while I’m typing this blog.

She must not know the following project details as this will be my suprise gift for her.

I will launch a website with customized domain name, hosted on github. In the website, I will do some cool stuff (TBD, but have some cool ideas) to impress her. The website will be on http://wes-ivy.me , which currently redirects to this blog.

How likely she will find out about this intent? Hmm, statistically highly unlikely. She is just a normal girl who can’t distinguish Mac OS & iOS =P

This project will be a whole learning experience for me as well. I’ve done Bootstrap before, but to really do what I have in mind requires far more than playing around.

I have 4 months, lets go and see.

Will post more updates later.
