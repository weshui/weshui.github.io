Amazon Fire Phone

Jun 19th, 2014

Spent hours watching Amazon event live today with CNET.

Gotta say Fire Phone’s dynamic perceptive is pretty “Amazing” at first glance. Imaging you can show off that to your friends =D

Very cool at first glance, right?

Except that, I don’t quite see many “Amazing” things. Firefly? the app that can recognize everything? Well, apparently I don’t want to buy everything from Amazon =D Joking around, the app is clearly to promote to Shop on Amazon, which I don’t quite agree that most people will buy. Maybe its a whole new experience for Amazon-mania, but Amazon is not Apple.

Jump to it’s price, 300 bucks for a contract phone just to showoff 3D Lock Screen? Let me think… Nope

Nice try Mr. Benzos. But I will switch to iPhone 6 most likely.
