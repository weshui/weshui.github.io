After Roadtrip

Sep 11th, 2014

Well, it’s been a while without blogging anything. The reason for that is, of course, ROAD TRIP.

I have to keep this blogging thing away from my girlfriend. Otherwise, it won’t be a suprise gift. Right?

Couple of updates.

California is a beautiful place.

I’ve been to San Fransico and L.A before, but I didn’t rent a car back then. This time, we rent car and visited Yosemite (yeah, next version of Mac OS X). In general, CA is an awesome place.

For those of you haven’t get a chance to really appreciate CA, this video is good for you.

I passed the interview, but no offer? What? Seriously?

I actually interviewed with CapitalOne for a Data Engineer role in their plano office. The first day after interview, my point of contact told me I passed the interview and congrats. Cool. But two days after, she said they need to make an exception to give me offer because my situation is not expected (OPT/F1). Well, why did you interview me in the first place?

New AppleEvent

Hmm, I will not get Apple Watch. 350$ for a not that impressive watch. Not worth it. But iPhone 6 is actually something. The power of NFC is “Massive” by one of my colleages. If Apple will release a NFC SDK, it will be Apple’s Victory.

Lastly, I just want to see what’s the reaction of my girlfriend once she sees this website.
