First Post

Jun 2nd, 2014

Yes, this is actually my first post.

I was never meant to be a guy who keeps track of eveything in his daily life. I thought the first impression others could/should have are “wtf” or “who cares”. Sadly, it didn’t change until recently thanks to one of my friends.

So, I started this, and as a “brogrammer” I believe it’s right of me to “code” my blog rather than write my blog.

For those who don’t have enough knowledge in programming or whats-so-ever (lucky you), I use octopress, github and sublime text 2 to make all this blog stuff happens (Ya, I know you have no idea what I’m talking about =D).

To make it short, I wanna keep track of my life so at least I can have some references to write autobiography when I’m too old to code and/or I can pull up some cool stuff as evidence when talking bs to my kids/grandkids (Hope world peace until then).

That’s the whole point of this Journal/Blog/Diary stuff.

And, of cource, to practice English. Yes people, I know more than Java, Ruby, C++, Python and Javascript!
